Mosaic Chateau Emperador Glazed Mixed Material (Stone and Glass) Mosaic Random Indoor/Outdoor Wall Tile (Common: 12-in x 12-in; Actual: 11.75-in x 13-in)

  • Random rectangular glass and marble mosaic tiles mounted on a 12-in x 12-in sheet
  • Suitable for exterior walls in freezing and non-freezing climates when proper installation methods are followed
  • Please inspect tile prior to installation, no adjustments will be allowed after installation
  • Recommended for residential walls and backsplashes
  • Recommended grout joints: 1/8-in
